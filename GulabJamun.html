Gulab Jamun

Description

Gulab Jamun is a classic Pakistani dessert and is made of milk solids which are deep fried and then immersed in a sugar syrup.

The following ingridents are required to make this delicious treat

  • 2 cups of water
  • White sugar
  • 1/2 Tablespoon Cardimon
  • 1/2 Dry Milk(Carnation)
  • 2 Tablespoon of All-purpose Flour
  • 1/4 Teaspoon of baking soda
  • 1 Tablespoon of Unsalted Butter
  • 2 Tablespoon of Plain Yougart
  • 2 Cups of Vegitable Oil
Directions
  1. In saucepan mix in the combination of water, Cardimon, sugar. Reduce the heat and let the combination simmer until it gets to a saucy type texture.
  2. Now mix the milk powder, flour, and baking soda in a seprate bowl unitl it is well-combinded. Slowly mix the Yougart and butter until a comes togther. Let the dough rest for 5-10mins.
  3. Now shape the dough in small balls.
  4. In saucepan heat oil at 350 Degrees. You can test if the oil is hot enough by dropping one Gulab Jamun in the sauce pan, and if the oils starts to boil up then the oil is ready.
  5. Start by frying 4 Gulab Jamun, and keep frying them until they get dark golden brown.
