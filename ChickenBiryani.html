Chicken Biryani

Description

Chicken Biryani is a savory chicken and rice dish that includes layers of chicken, rice, and aromatics that are steamed together. The bottom layer of rice absorbs all the chicken juices as it cooks, giving it a tender texture and rich flavor, while the top layer of rice turns out white and fluffy. Buried in the biryani, you’ll find whole cuts of succulent chicken bursting with flavor from the potent array of spices, herbs, and aromatics it’s marinated in.

Rice Ingredients

  • 2 cups basmati rice
  • 2 to 2 ½ litres water
  • 1 bay leaf , small
  • 4 cloves , optional
Curry Ingredients
  • 3 tbsp oil
  • 2 onions medium, sliced thinly
  • 1 star anise
  • 3 green cardamoms
  • 1 black cardamom (optional)
  • 4 cloves
  • 1 inch cinnamon stick
  • ½ tsp shahi jeera
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 1 tbsp ginger garlic paste
  • ½ kg chicken (500 to 600 grams) (or 1 lb)
  • Salt as needed
  • 1/8 to ¼ tsp turmeric
  • 1 ½ to 2 tsp biryani masala
  • ¾ to 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 2 green chilies slit (adjust as needed)
  • 1 cup yogurt
  • 1 Handful mint finely chopped
  • 1 Handful coriander leaves finely chopped
Method to Make Biriyani Rice
  1. Wash rice few times until the water runs clear. Soak in ample amount of water for 30 mins. Drain the water.
  2. Bring 2 to 2 ½ liters water to a boil in a pot or a cooker Add bay leaf and cloves.
  3. When the water comes to a boil, add salt and drained rice.
  4. Cook until it is done. Rice should be firm, grainy and you must feel the bite into the grain when you bite it. Drain it off to a colander.
Method to Make Curry
  1. Heat 3 tbsp. oil in a pan add sliced onions and fry evenly until golden.
  2. When they turn dark brown, set aside ¼ portion of these fried onions.
  3. To the rest of the onions, add 1 small bay leaf, star anise, ½ tsp shahi jeera, 3 cardamoms, 4 cloves. Saute just for a min without burning.
  4. Add 1 tbsp. ginger garlic paste.
  5. Saute on a medium flame until the raw smell of ginger garlic goes off.
  6. Add chicken.
  7. Saute for 2 to 3 mins until the chicken turns white in color. Sprinkle salt.
  8. Add biryani masala, red chili powder, turmeric and green chilies.
  9. Mix everything well and fry for 3 mins. After that cover and cook on a low flame for 3 to 4 mins for the chicken to release the moisture.
  10. Add 1 cup yogurt.
  11. Stir, cover and cook on a medium flame until chicken is soft and tender.
  12. When the chicken is cooked completely, a thick gravy is left in the pot with oil separated.
  13. Check the salt now and add more if needed. Stir well and sprinkle chopped coriander leaves.
How to Layer Chicken Biryani
  1. Use a separate pot. Just before layering the rice, set aside half of the chicken curry. Layer half the rice. Then layer the rest of the curry. Repeat layering rice then mint coriander and fried onions.
  2. Cover the pot with a foil.
  3. Cover with a air tight lid to trap the steam or dum. If you do not have a tight lid then you can place a heavy pot over the lid.
  4. Put the Pot in oven at 350c for 40 to 45 minutes.
  5. Aromatic Chicken Biriyani is ready.
