Chicken Biryani

Description

Chicken Biryani is one of the most famous in Pakistani cuisine. Chicken Biyrani consits of having cooked chicken curry at the bottom and steamed rice on the top. What makes Biryani so famous is its magnificent aroma of the spieces. The chicken Biryani is usually cooked into seprate phases. In the first phase you are required to cook the rice, and in the second you cook the Chicken curry.

Ingridents

  • 3 cups of Basmati rice
  • 2Pounds of chicken
  • 1 Teaspoon of Salt
  • 1/2 Cumin Powder
  • 1 tablespoon Garam Masala
  • 1 Teaspoon of Chili powder
  • 1 Teaspoon of Corriander Powder
  • 1 Teaspoon of Cumin Powder
  • 1/2 Tumeric
  • 1/4 citric Acid

To make the tardional Chicken Biryani

Method
  1. In big saucepan take 3 cups of Bastmasti rice and soak in the water.
  2. In the drained rice add 10 cups of water.
  3. Add 3 teaspoons of salt in the water.
  4. In the saucepan add 2 bayleaves, 1/2 tablespoon of cummin powder, and 4 peieces of Cardamom.
  5. Now let the rice cook on low-medium heat.
  6. When the rice are 50% cooked drain the water out and pour the rice on the chicken curry.
  7. When you put all the rice into the chicken.

Now its time to cook the curry

  1. In saucepan add 2pounds of chicken
  2. Add in the mixture of Chicken Poweder, Garam Masala, Chili Powder, Corriander Powder, Cumin Powder, Salt, Tumeric, and Citric Acid.
  3. Now add 1 Teaspoon of Ginger garlic paste.
  4. To give that kick to the overall dish add Chaat Masala, also add 1/2 cup of Fried Onions.
  5. Now add in the combination of Black Cardamom, 4 Green Cardamom, 2 Bay Leaves, 2 Star Anise, and 1/2 Teaspoon of Black Cumin.
  6. Add 1/2 cup of Tomato puree inside the mixture.
  7. Now add in the combination of 1/2 Handul of Chopped Mint, 1 Green Chili pepper, 1 Handful Cilantro, and add 2 Tablespoon of lemon
  8. Now mix all the ingridents with the chicken
  9. Add 6 tablespoon of oil
  10. Now let's go back the cooked rice.
